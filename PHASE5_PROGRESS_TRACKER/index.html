    Decision 1: Implementation Approach¶

    Date: November 23, 2025
    Decision: Use Pyodide-based approach for MVP (Phase 5.1)
    Rationale: - Faster time to market (10 weeks vs 6 months for native rewrite) - Reuse entire existing Python codebase - Can optimize with native WASM compiler later (Phase 5.2)

    Alternatives Considered: - Native WASM compiler (Rust/C++) - rejected due to timeline
