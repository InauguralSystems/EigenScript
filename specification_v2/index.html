    4.1 Complete Grammar (BNF)¶

    <program>      ::= <statement>*

<statement>    ::= <assignment>

<primary>      ::= <literal>
                 | <identifier>

<definition>   ::= "define" <identifier> "as" ":" <block>

<conditional>  ::= "if" <expression> ":" <block> ("else" ":" <block>)?

<vector>       ::= "(" <number> ("," <number>)* ")"
